
## 1. Production outage with strict response time

A financial services company runs a **mission-critical production workload** on AWS. During a peak business hour, the application becomes unavailable and the team needs **immediate access to AWS engineers** with a **15-minute response time**.

Which AWS Support plan should the company use?

**A.** Basic Support <br>
**B.** Developer Support <br>
**C.** Business Support <br>
**D.** Enterprise Support <br>

✅ **Correct Answer: D. Enterprise Support**

### Explanation:

* **Enterprise Support** provides:

  * **15-minute response time** for **Business-Critical System Down** issues
  * Access to a **Technical Account Manager (TAM)**
* Business Support has a **1-hour response time**, which does not meet the requirement.

## 2. Cost-effective support for non-production workloads

A startup runs **development and testing environments** on AWS. The team wants **email-based technical support** during business hours but wants to **minimize costs**.

Which support plan is MOST appropriate?

**A.** Basic Support <br>
**B.** Developer Support <br>
**C.** Business Support <br>
**D.** Enterprise Support <br>

✅ **Correct Answer: B. Developer Support**

### Explanation:

* **Developer Support** is designed for:

  * Non-production workloads
  * Email access to Cloud Support Associates
  * Lower cost compared to Business and Enterprise plans
* Basic Support does **not include technical support**.

## 3. Access to AWS Trusted Advisor checks

A solutions architect wants access to **AWS Trusted Advisor checks for security, performance, and cost optimization** beyond the basic service limits.

Which support plan should be recommended?

**A.** Basic Support <br>
**B.** Developer Support <br>
**C.** Business Support <br>
**D.** Enterprise Support <br>

✅ **Correct Answer: C. Business Support**

### Explanation:

* **Business Support and above** provide:

  * **Full Trusted Advisor checks**
* Basic and Developer plans provide **limited checks only**.

## 4. Guidance for architecture and cost optimization

A company wants AWS experts to:

* Review architecture
* Provide **best practices**
* Assist with **cost optimization**
* Respond to production issues within **1 hour**

Which support plan meets these requirements at the **lowest cost**?

**A.** Developer Support <br>
**B.** Business Support <br>
**C.** Enterprise Support <br>
**D.** Basic Support <br>

✅ **Correct Answer: B. Business Support**

### Explanation:

* **Business Support** offers:

  * 24/7 phone, chat, and email support
  * **1-hour response time** for production issues
  * Architectural and cost optimization guidance
* Enterprise adds TAM but is more expensive and unnecessary here.

## 6. Understanding what Basic Support provides

A new AWS customer wants:

* Access to documentation
* Access to AWS forums
* Ability to open billing and account-related cases

Which support plan satisfies these requirements?

**A.** Basic Support <br>
**B.** Developer Support <br>
**C.** Business Support <br>
**D.** Enterprise Support <br>

✅ **Correct Answer: A. Basic Support**

### Explanation:

* **Basic Support (free)** includes:

  * 24/7 access to customer service for **billing and account issues**
  * Documentation and whitepapers
  * AWS forums
* No technical support is included.
